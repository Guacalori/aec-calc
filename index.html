Getting Started

Find each appliance’s power rating (in watts or kW), how long you use it daily, and your electricity rate (from your bill).

Input the appliance name, power, usage time, and rate into the calculator.

Click “Calculate” to see energy use and cost per month.

  4. Review Results

Compare appliances to spot which use the most electricity.

Unplug unused devices, use efficient appliances, and adjust usage times.
